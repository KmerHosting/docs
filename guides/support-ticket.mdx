title: "Open a ticket"
description: "Contact support with the information needed to resolve an issue faster."
icon: "message-circle"
---

A clear ticket reduces diagnosis time.

## Information to include

- Account email.
- Affected service.
- Related domain.
- Order or invoice ID.
- Exact error message.
- Screenshot if useful.
- Approximate date and time of the issue.
- Troubleshooting steps already attempted.

## Example ticket

```text
Hello,

My domain example.com stopped pointing to my website around 14:30.
Account: client@example.com
Service: Shared hosting
Recent action: updated the DNS A record
Visible error: the browser shows DNS_PROBE_FINISHED_NXDOMAIN

Can you check the DNS zone and the service status?
```
