---
title: "Auto-renewal"
description: "Enable or disable automatic renewal."
icon: "repeat"
---

Auto-renewal helps avoid service or domain expiration.

## General conditions

- The service must be eligible.
- The account must be in good standing.
- Account credit must be sufficient at renewal time.
- Billing information must be correct.

## Change the setting

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the service">
    From the customer area, select the related service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open auto-renewal">
    Review the current status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable or disable">
    Save the new configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the credit balance">
    Make sure the balance covers the next renewal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
