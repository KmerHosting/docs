Auto-renewal controls whether a supported service should attempt renewal at its renewal boundary.

<Frame caption="The service settings modal exposes the current auto-renewal state.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/auto-renewal-toggle.png" alt="The service settings modal exposes the current auto-renewal state." />
</Frame>


## Before enabling

- Confirm the account credit or payment method can cover the expected renewal.
- Review the renewal price and currency.
- Confirm the service should remain active beyond the current term.
- Keep expiry notifications enabled.

## Before disabling

Understand the suspension, expiration, deletion, and recovery policy for the service. Disabling auto-renewal does not necessarily cancel the service immediately; it changes the next renewal behavior.

## API and CLI

Authorized developer keys can update auto-renewal through the Developer API, SDK, or CLI for supported service types.
