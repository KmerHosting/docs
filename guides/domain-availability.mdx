---
title: "Check domain availability"
description: "Look up domain availability before purchase."
icon: "search"
---

Domain availability tells you whether a name can still be registered for a specific extension.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open domain search">
    From the Pricing page or checkout, use the domain search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the full name">
    Example: `example.com` or `example.cm`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the result">
    If available, add the domain to the cart. Otherwise choose another extension or name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the purchase quickly">
    An available domain can be purchased by someone else before your payment is completed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
