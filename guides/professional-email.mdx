Professional Email is currently included through Shared Hosting. The KmerHosting console lists eligible domains, while mailbox creation occurs in DirectAdmin.

<Frame caption="Confirm the domain belongs to an active Shared Hosting plan before creating the mailbox.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/professional-email-list.png" alt="Confirm the domain belongs to an active Shared Hosting plan before creating the mailbox." />
</Frame>


## Create the mailbox

1. Open the Shared Hosting service.
2. Open DirectAdmin.
3. Go to email account management.
4. Choose a mailbox such as `support@example.com`.
5. Generate a unique password.
6. Save the mailbox and quota settings.

<Frame caption="Use a fictional mailbox and hide the password in documentation captures.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/directadmin-email-account.png" alt="Use a fictional mailbox and hide the password in documentation captures." />
</Frame>


## Open Roundcube

Use the link exposed by the Professional Email page or open the configured Roundcube URL. Sign in with the complete email address.

<Frame caption="Roundcube requires the complete mailbox address and its mailbox-specific password.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/roundcube-login.png" alt="Roundcube requires the complete mailbox address and its mailbox-specific password." />
</Frame>


## Recommended mailboxes

- `hello@domain`
- `support@domain`
- `billing@domain`
- `security@domain`

Use aliases and forwarding only after the primary mailbox behavior is understood.
