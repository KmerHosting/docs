Checkout turns the cart into a payable order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the cart">
    Confirm the service, billing term, domain, options, and final amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the profile">
    Make sure account and billing information is correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the order">
    Confirm the checkout. An order and sometimes an invoice are generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    Pay using account credit or another supported method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track activation">
    Follow the order and the service from the customer area.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## If payment fails

- Check the account credit balance.
- Confirm the exact amount.
- Verify whether KYC or email verification is required.
- Open a ticket with the order reference.
