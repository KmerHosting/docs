Active services are available from the customer area. Depending on the service type, you can review settings, renew, manage the related domain, or request support actions.

## Common actions

| Action | Where to do it |
|---|---|
| View details | Service page in the customer area. |
| Renew | Renewal or order section. |
| Manage the domain | Domains page or related service page. |
| View server settings | Service page or activation email. |
| Request a change | Support ticket. |

<Note>
For sensitive services, some actions may require additional account verification.
</Note>
