Service pages expose different controls depending on the service type.

## Common information

- Service type and tier.
- Active or pending state.
- Purchase and billing currency.
- Activation and renewal dates.
- Associated domains.
- Credentials or provider access when applicable.
- Renewal and auto-renewal controls.

## Credential handling

Passwords and tokens should be revealed only when needed, stored in a password manager, and rotated after accidental exposure. Screenshots must cover every secret value.

## Support context

Include the service ID, service type, domain or hostname, timestamp, and exact error. Do not send the root password or API key.
