This page explains how to request a refund. Exact eligibility depends on the official policy.

## Official document

See the official version at `https://kmerhosting.com/refund-policy`.

## Before opening a request

Prepare:

- the account email;
- the order or invoice ID;
- the affected service;
- the date of the event;
- useful evidence without passwords or secrets.
