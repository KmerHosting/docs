## Manual renewal

Open the service or domain, choose the renewal action, review the duration and currency, and confirm only when sufficient account credit is available.

<Frame caption="Domain renewal is managed from the standalone-domain controls when available.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/domain-renewal.png" alt="Domain renewal is managed from the standalone-domain controls when available." />

## Auto-renewal

Auto-renewal is a preference, not a guarantee that a renewal will succeed. The wallet must have sufficient credit and the service must remain eligible.

## Before expiry

- Keep expiry notifications enabled.
- Review the current renewal price.
- Add account credit early enough for payment processing.
- Verify the service state after renewal.
