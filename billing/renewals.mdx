A renewal extends the lifetime of a service or domain. It can be manual or automatic depending on the configuration.

## Manual renewal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the service">
    From the customer area, select the service or domain to renew.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the term">
    Confirm the duration and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the order">
    Confirm the renewal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    Pay using account credit or a supported method.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-renewal

Auto-renewal lets a service renew automatically when the required conditions are met, especially when sufficient account credit is available.

<Warning>
If account credit is insufficient, auto-renewal can fail. Monitor expiration dates closely.
</Warning>
