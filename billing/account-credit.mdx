Account credit is an internal balance attached to the customer account. It can be used to pay for an order or renewal when the balance is sufficient.

## Manual top-up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request the instructions">
    From the customer area, open the credit section or ask for top-up instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the payment">
    Send the exact amount using a supported payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit proof">
    Share the payment proof along with the account email and payment reference.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for validation">
    Support verifies the payment and then credits the account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Do not publish or share private payment references in a public ticket. Use official channels only.
</Warning>
