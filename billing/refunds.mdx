Refunds depend on the official policy, the service type, the time since purchase, and the service usage state.

## Before requesting a refund

Check:

- the order ID;
- the invoice ID;
- the affected service;
- the payment date;
- the payment method;
- the reason for the request.

## Open a request

Open a ticket from `https://kmerhosting.com/open-ticket` with the required details.

<Note>
See the official policy at `https://kmerhosting.com/refund-policy`. This documentation page summarizes the process but does not replace the legal policy.
</Note>
