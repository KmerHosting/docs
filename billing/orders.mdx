An order is created when the cart is confirmed. It may cover hosting, VPS, a domain, a renewal, or a custom service.

## Create an order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add to cart">
    Select the offer, billing term, options, and domain if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the cart">
    Check the amount, term, domain, and options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the order">
    Validate the order from the checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay">
    Use account credit or follow the payment instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Possible statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Order created but not paid or not fully validated. |
| Paid | Payment accepted. |
| Processing | Activation or execution in progress. |
| Completed | Service delivered or renewal applied. |
| Cancelled | Order cancelled or expired. |
