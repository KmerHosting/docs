Invoices help track amounts due, recorded payments, and the documents linked to your services.

## Access invoices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the customer area">
    Sign in to `https://kmerhosting.com/customers`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to billing">
    Open the invoices or orders section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the document">
    Use the download button when the PDF is available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common statuses

| Status | Action |
|---|---|
| Draft | Wait for final generation. |
| Pending | Pay the invoice. |
| Paid | Keep the receipt. |
| Cancelled | Do not pay without support confirmation. |
