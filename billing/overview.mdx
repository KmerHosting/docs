## Core records

- **Account credit:** wallet balance used by supported checkout and renewal operations.
- **Order:** requested items and total before or during payment.
- **Invoice:** billing document with paid, pending, or overdue state.
- **Service:** the activated or pending operational resource.
- **Incoming invoice:** renewal-related billing record shown by the console.

## Currency behavior

The platform supports regional price books and account billing context. A top-up can be displayed in the wallet currency while CamerPay settlement occurs in XAF. The platform stores enough context to credit the correct wallet amount after a confirmed webhook.
