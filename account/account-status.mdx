---
title: "Account status"
description: "Understand account statuses and the actions available for each state."
icon: "circle-user-round"
---

The account status affects access to orders, services, invoices, and certain sensitive actions.

| Status | Expected access | Recommended action |
|---|---|---|
| `unverified` | Limited access based on internal rules | Verify the email and complete the profile. |
| `submitted` | Access pending review | Wait for validation or reply to support. |
| `verified` | Normal access | Keep account details up to date. |
| `suspended` | Partial or blocked access | Contact support. |
| `disabled` | Access disabled | Contact support with justification. |
| `terminated` | Account closed | Open a new request if applicable. |

<Note>
Exact rules may vary depending on service type, order risk, and payment status.
</Note>
