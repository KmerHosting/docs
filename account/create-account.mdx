A customer account is required to order hosting, buy domains, review invoices, track services, and open support tickets.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Go to `https://kmerhosting.com/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Use an active email address, a unique password, and accurate contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    If an error appears, fix the highlighted fields and submit again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the email address">
    Confirm the account email before placing orders or managing a service.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best practices

- Use a unique password.
- Do not share account credentials.
- Keep the email address up to date.
- Add accurate billing details to avoid delays.

<Warning>
Support will never ask for your password. If someone does, do not share it.
</Warning>
