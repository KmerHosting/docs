A pending service has passed the order creation stage but is not yet active in the customer console.

## What to do

- Wait for normal provisioning when the service was just purchased.
- Confirm that the related order and invoice are paid.
- Avoid creating a duplicate order for the same workload.
- Contact support with the order number, service type, and purchase timestamp if the state remains pending unexpectedly.
