The Active Services page aggregates services that are currently active for the authenticated user.

## Open the dedicated service page

Selecting a service routes to the matching console section. Use the dedicated page for credentials, domains, settings, and renewal controls.

## Data interpretation

- **Activation date** is when the service became operational.
- **Renewal date** is the next expected recurring boundary.
- **Purchase price** is stored with the billing currency captured for the service.
- **Service status** is the platform state and may differ from a provider panel state during a transition.
