## Platform boundaries

- `kmerhosting.com` hosts public pages, checkout, payment returns, and the existing web backend.
- `console.kmerhosting.com` hosts the authenticated customer experience while sharing the same platform session.
- `admin.kmerhosting.com` is an internal administration console and is not part of the public customer API.
- The official documentation is published under `kmerhosting.com/docs` from the public `KmerHosting/docs` repository.
