## Homepage shows a profile but console returns to login

1. Sign out.
2. Remove site data for `kmerhosting.com` and `console.kmerhosting.com`.
3. Open a private window.
4. Sign in again.
5. Confirm the session cookie is scoped to the KmerHosting parent domain, uses path `/`, and is Secure and HttpOnly.

## Other checks

- Complete email verification.
- Complete onboarding when redirected.
- Confirm the browser allows cookies.
- Disable extensions that rewrite cookies or redirects for the test.
- Check the account status card.
