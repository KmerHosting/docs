## Quick checks

- Verify the email address being used.
- Reset the password.
- Confirm that the account email is verified.
- Try a private browsing window.
- Temporarily disable extensions that block cookies.

## Contact support

Include the account email, the exact error message, and the approximate time of the failed attempt.
