## Common causes

- Incorrect DNS record.
- Incomplete DNS propagation.
- Wrong nameservers.
- Expired domain.
- SSL certificate not validated.

## What to send to support

- Affected domain.
- Records that were changed.
- Screenshot of the DNS manager.
- Expected result.
- Date and time of the change.
