This guide covers the standard customer journey: account creation, verification, ordering, payment, activation, and first deployment steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the account">
    Go to `https://kmerhosting.com/register`, enter a working email address, and complete the initial profile details carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the email">
    Open the verification link from your inbox. If the link expires, request a fresh one from the login flow before opening a support request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the customer profile">
    Add billing identity details, contact information, and organization data if the purchase is for a company.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish KYC when requested">
    Some services may require identity review before activation or before higher-risk changes are approved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the right service">
    Shared hosting fits standard websites and WordPress. VPS fits custom runtimes, root access, workers, and container-based deployments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place the order and pay">
    Review the plan, create the order, and complete payment with the supported payment path shown in the client area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the service after activation">
    Open the client area and check the service details for credentials, assigned resources, and related domains.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the correct deployment guide">
    Follow <a href="/guides/shared-hosting-stacks">shared hosting deployment recipes</a> or <a href="/guides/vps-stacks">VPS deployment recipes</a> based on the service you purchased.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When you contact support, include the account email, domain, order ID, invoice ID, and a short description of what you expected to happen.
</Tip>
