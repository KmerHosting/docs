## Current workflow

1. Own or attach a domain to an active Shared Hosting service.
2. Open the DirectAdmin panel for that hosting account.
3. Create the mailbox in DirectAdmin.
4. Open Roundcube from the KmerHosting console or the webmail URL.
5. Sign in with the complete email address and the mailbox password.

## Important boundary

The KmerHosting Professional Email page does not create the mailbox directly. It lists eligible hosted domains and provides access to Roundcube. Mailbox creation and password changes happen in DirectAdmin.
