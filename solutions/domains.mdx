## Standalone domains

Standalone domain cards can show registration and renewal prices, registration and expiry dates, auto-renewal state, nameservers, transfer lock, DNS records, and renewal controls.

<Frame caption="A standalone domain includes commercial and DNS information in one service card.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/standalone-domain-card.png" alt="A standalone domain includes commercial and DNS information in one service card." />
</Frame>


## Included domains

Included domains are associated with Shared Hosting, Tiny VPS, VPS, or Custom Hosting records. The available controls focus on nameservers and DNS. The exact included-domain entitlement depends on the purchased hosting plan.

<Frame caption="Included domains remain linked to the hosting service that granted them.">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/included-domain-card.png" alt="Included domains remain linked to the hosting service that granted them." />
</Frame>


## Supported DNS record types

The current editor recognizes `A`, `AAAA`, `CNAME`, `MX`, `TXT`, `NS`, `SRV`, and `CAA` records. Use valid values and allow for DNS propagation after changes.
