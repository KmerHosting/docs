Custom hosting is designed for customers whose needs are not covered by standard offers: special configurations, migrations, managed operations, dedicated resources, application-specific environments, or technical guidance.

## Example requests

- Migration of an existing website.
- Setup of a control panel or application stack.
- Deployment for Node.js, Laravel, WordPress, Python, or Docker.
- Security-focused assistance.
- Backup or recovery planning.
- Multi-service architecture.

## Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe the requirement">
    Provide the domain, expected traffic, technology stack, criticality, and desired timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive a proposal">
    KmerHosting reviews the request and proposes a suitable configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the order">
    After approval, the order and billing are created.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track activation">
    Support shares credentials or the next steps depending on the service scope.
  </Step>
</Steps>
