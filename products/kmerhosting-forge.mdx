## Intended direction

- Connect deployments to source repositories.
- Prepare and update application stacks.
- Surface service health and deployment state.
- Reduce repeated manual server setup for supported workloads.

<Note>
Do not document Forge as generally available until the console exposes a complete, tested deployment workflow. The current page is a product preview.
</Note>
