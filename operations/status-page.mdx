The status page is used to communicate incidents, maintenance windows, and service degradation.

## Recommended services to monitor

- Public website.
- Customer area.
- API and checkout.
- Shared hosting services.
- VPS services.
- DNS and email.
- Payments and billing.

## Where to check

Visit `https://status.kmerhosting.com` when a service appears unavailable.

<Note>
If the status page does not mention your issue, open a ticket with the relevant technical details.
</Note>
