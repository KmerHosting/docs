title: "Incidents"
description: "Interpret service incidents and provide useful account-specific evidence."
icon: "siren"
---


An incident is an unplanned degradation or outage affecting one or more platform components.

## During an incident

- Avoid repeated destructive retries.
- Preserve exact error messages and timestamps.
- Check whether payments, provisioning, or DNS changes may complete later.
- Do not create duplicate orders or top-ups without verifying state.
- Follow the status-page updates.

## After recovery

Verify account credit, invoice state, order state, service activation, DNS, and application health. Contact support when an account-specific record remains inconsistent.
