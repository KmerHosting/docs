Planned maintenance allows updates, migrations, optimizations, or security operations to be applied safely.

## Information to review

- Affected service.
- Date and time.
- Estimated duration.
- Expected impact.
- Required customer actions.

## Before a critical maintenance window

- Back up important data.
- Avoid DNS or application changes close to the window.
- Warn your users if the application might be affected.
